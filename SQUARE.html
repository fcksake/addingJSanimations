AMERICAN EXPRESS EXPERIENCE

I assisted in the CAD and fabrication of the American Express Cardholder experience for the 2018 Panorama Festival. This was by far one of the largest projects I have ever been involved in and it was not at all a singular effort. I got to work with a large team of artists and fabricators in the design and assembly of this space.
