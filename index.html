Recent Projects

take a look at what I've been up to these past few years.

"Organic Xanax" Embedded Fashion

Hardware Design, Prototyping, Soft Manufacturing

Produced and wrote custom hardware and softwware for garments shown at Lunar Gala 2020. This was an entirely self-guided and motivativated project intended to integrate electronics into clothing seamlessly while maintaining a light hearted and humorous concept.

AI Invaders/Defenders (2019)

Hardware, AI Design Research

Playful exploration of the role of artificial intelligence in our lives. Utlizes GANbreeder to manipulate faces in the context of an arcade game. Custom hardware, software, mock ups and branding. Games act as the physical representation of GANs, and exist within an ecosysten intended to educate the user about the capabilities of AI.

Autodesk 3D Research (2018-2019)

CAD, Computational Design

Using Fusion360, pushed the exploration of the sculpt environment. This was done at the Computational Engineering and Robotics Lab where we researched the bounds of variable density for customizable footwear.

Walt Disney Imagineering R&D (2018)

Creative Technology Internship

Supported hardware prototypes and manufacturing. Pushed my software abilities and learned hands on what it takes to create high fidelity experiences. Additionally worked heavily on an AR/VR visualization tool.
